# Project Idea: Palmidos New Landing Page

## Description
Create a modern, compelling landing page for Palmidos AI - a software development agency specializing in AI solutions. The landing page should take inspiration from palmidos.com while presenting a fresh, AI-focused positioning.

## Brand Essence (from palmidos.com)
**Tagline:** "TECHNOLOGY, CREATIVITY, PEOPLE."
**Mission:** Palmidos is your partner in building lasting tech solutions for your business.

## Core Value Proposition for AI Focus
Palmidos AI helps businesses:
- Build custom AI agents and automation
- Integrate AI into existing workflows
- Develop AI-powered products from idea to launch
- Get expert AI consulting and strategy

## Why Choose Palmidos AI (Key Differentiators)
1. **End-to-End AI Development** — From concept to production deployment
2. **Custom AI Agents** — Tailored solutions, not one-size-fits-all
3. **Business-First Approach** — Technology that drives real ROI
4. **Rapid Prototyping** — See results in weeks, not months
5. **Expert Team** — Deep expertise in LLMs, automation, and integration

## Target Audience
- Startup founders wanting to add AI features
- Enterprise managers looking to automate processes
- Product teams needing AI integration
- Businesses seeking competitive advantage through AI

## Key Sections Needed
1. **Hero** — Bold headline with AI focus + CTA
2. **Why Palmidos AI** — The 5 differentiators above
3. **Services** — AI Development, AI Integration, AI Consulting, AI Agents
4. **Process/Flow** — How we work (Discovery → Design → Develop → Deploy)
5. **Social Proof** — Case studies or testimonials
6. **CTA Section** — "Got an idea? Let's make it together. Book a call."
7. **Footer** — Contact, social links, company info

## Design Direction
- Modern, clean, professional
- Tech-forward aesthetic
- Trust-building through clarity
- Mobile-first responsive
- Fast performance (Core Web Vitals)

## Technical Stack
- Next.js 14+ (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- Framer Motion for animations
- Vercel deployment
- Neon PostgreSQL (for contact form)

## Inspiration Source
palmidos.com — "TECHNOLOGY, CREATIVITY, PEOPLE." ethos
